## Option 1: Change Project Name (Easiest - Changes Default Domain)

### Steps:
1. Go to Vercel Dashboard
2. Click on your project: `willyoubemyvalentine`
3. Go to: **Settings** ‚Üí **General**
4. Find: **Project Name**
5. Change it to something shorter, like:
   - `valentine`
   - `vday`
   - `match`
   - `campus`
   - `love`
6. Click **Save**
7. Your new domain will be: `https://your-new-name.vercel.app`

**Note**: This will automatically update your deployment URL!

## Option 2: Add Custom Domain (If You Have One)

### Steps:
1. Go to: **Settings** ‚Üí **Domains**
2. Click **Add Domain**
3. Enter your domain (e.g., `valentine.app`, `vday.me`, etc.)
4. Follow the DNS configuration instructions
5. Wait for DNS propagation (usually 5-30 minutes)
